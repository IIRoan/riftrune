# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Riftbound collectors and players using the site on mobile at shops, events, and trade tables, or on desktop while sorting binders, checking prices, and comparing printings. The primary user is trying to find the right card fast, understand its play details, and make a confident collecting or trading decision.

## Product Purpose

riftrune.com is a card catalog and market reference for Riftbound. It should make card discovery, card details, variant comparison, and price checks feel fast, trustworthy, and pleasant across mobile and web.

## Brand Personality

Smooth, precise, and collectible. The interface should borrow the calm utility of Airbnb and Uber: obvious controls, polished transitions, and confident hierarchy, while still carrying enough Riftbound personality to avoid feeling like a generic SaaS template.

## Anti-references

No gamer neon, no overbuilt fantasy chrome, no noisy card-game UI, no generic dashboard palette, and no marketing landing page that blocks users from the catalog. Motion should feel responsive and premium, not theatrical.

## Design Principles

Start in the task: search, browse, and inspect cards should be visible immediately.

Collector confidence: prices, printings, rarity, set, and ownership cues need clear hierarchy and stable formatting.

Player speed: rules text, cost, type, color, and stats should be scan-friendly with familiar affordances.

Smooth restraint: use motion for state changes and orientation only, with reduced-motion support.

Personality through artifacts: let real card imagery, game symbols, and precise microcopy carry the brand instead of decorative effects.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast. Support keyboard focus, readable text sizes, clear hit targets, reduced-motion preferences, and color-independent status labels for rarity, ownership, and market states.
